Below mentioned destinations are some of the best places to travel in January that have been handpicked to make winter season more magical.     1. Phuket

    Phuket is one of the best destinations in January. The golden beaches, the bustling beach shacks, the exhilarating water sports, and the amazing nightlife in Phuket must not be missed. And for the newlyweds, there’s nothing better than a Phuket honeymoon holiday.

    Places To Eat: Raya Restaurant, Suay Restaurant, La Gaetana Restaurant
    Top Tourist Attractions: Old Phuket Town, Soi Bangla Road, Patong Beach, Phi Phi, Koh Panyee, Kata Noi Beach, James Bond Island
    Things to do: Jungle safari, scuba diving, snorkelling, and water surfing
    Average Price: INR 2,000-2,500 per day per person
    Weather: Warm and Dry weather, 23°C to 31°C
    Places To Stay At: Blu Monkey Hub and Hotel Phuket, Borbaboom Phuket Poshtel & Hostel
    How to reach: Although there are no direct trains to Phuket, one can travel on trains going from Bangkok to Singapore and get off at Phun Phin railway station. Phuket is another 5 hours by bus from Phun Phin, which is located in the Surat Thani Province of Thailand.

    2. Bali

    Bali is a year-round destination, but for the travelers seeking a peaceful vacation in the Island of Gods, January is just the perfect time. This place is also considered to be one of the best places to visit in January for honeymoon. In this month most of the tourist crowd is cleared out and gone home; which means you have quieter beaches, and competitive hotel rates to look forward to. Bali in January is worth experiencing and the travelers can witness the best side of the island during this time.

    Places To Visit: Kuta, Ubud, Seminyak, Gili
    Places To Eat: Mauri Restaurant, Warong Legong, Kubu at Mandapa
    Top Tourist Attractions: Tanah Lot, Uluwatu Temple, Rice Terraces, and Mount Batur
    Things to do: Surfing, temple hopping, Bali swing
    Average Price: INR 1,800 per day for a couple
    Weather: Warm and sunny, 23°C to 29°C
    Places To Stay At: Quest Hotel San Denpasar,Quest Hotel San Denpasar
    How to reach: By flight: Ngurah Rai International Airport or Denpasar International Airport is located in South Kuta district and is just 13 kilometres from Denpasar, the capital of Bali.

    3. Maldives

    Maldives, a picturesque island country, is one of the popular places for best trips in January. The assemblage of charming islands, crystal blue sea, sparkling beaches, and lofty reefs make a holiday in Maldives during the winter month fascinating and alluring. January is an ideal time to visit Maldives as one can enjoy the most enthralling water sports.

    Places To Visit: Male, Maafushi, Addu Atoll
    Places To Eat: The Embassy Restaurant & Bar, Paranda, Reethi Restaurant
    Top Tourist Attractions: Snorkeling, island hopping, Ithaa underwater aquarium restaurant
    Things to do: Snorkeling, island hopping, submarine ride, and fishing cruises
    Average Price:INR 5,000-7000 per day for a couple
    Weather: Winters and dry weather, 25°C to 29°C
    Places To Stay At: Kaani Beach Hotel, Arena Beach Hotel
    How to reach: There are also direct and chartered flights coming in from Europe, China, Dubai and Singapore. The airport is located on Hulhumale Island, just 3.5 km away from Male. It is the only international airport of Maldives. If you are traveling from India, direct flights are available from Kochi and Bangalore.

The breathtaking scenic splendour of the destination has made Seychelles tour packages highly famous.

    The dream skiing destination of the world, Switzerland is one of the best countries to visit in January for the snow lovers. The climate gets chilly, and snow turns the alps into a wonderland. There is so much to do in Switzerland, whether you’re here for the famed chocolates or Christmas markets, or the exciting dog sledding.

    Places To Visit In Switzerland: Zurich, Geneva, Lucerne, Verbier
    Places To Eat: Quai 61, Loft Five, Villa Schweizerhof
    Top Tourist Attractions: Jungfrau, Mount Titlis, Lake Lucerne, Staubbach Falls, Rhine Falls
    Things to do: Swimming in the Lake Lucerne, party at the clubs, visit Soglio village
    Average Price: INR 9,500 per day per person
    Weather: Winters, -2°C to 2°C
    Places To Stay At: Jugendherberge Engelberg, Seehotel Wilerbad
    How to reach: It’s advisable to fly into either Paris or Milan and then fly to any of the major Swiss airports in case you don’t find a direct flight. India and Switzerland are thoroughly connected by many direct flights and the minimum time to get to Switzerland is just 7 hours.

The long list of places to visit in Cambodia include fabulous temples, serene beaches, and breathtaking landscapes that are worth exploring, especially during the winter month of January. The weather is pleasant; neither too hot nor too cold. Still wondering where to travel in January?

    Places To Visit: Siem Reap, Phnom Penh, Tonle Sap
    Places To Eat: Embassy Khmer Gastronomy, Cuisine Wat Damnak, Marum
    Top Tourist Attractions: Kampot, Kratie, Angkor Wat
    Things to do: Cruising, and jungle safari in Virachey National

    12. Costa Rica

    The stunningly beautiful Costa Rica is one of the best places to visit in January in 2023. The luxuriant rainforest destination of Central America is peaceful & serene and is the home to a rich biodiversity.     22. Ethiopia

    Ethiopia is one of the best places to visit in January outside India which is known for its beautiful highlands and the age-old traditions which are still followed by the locals. The best time of the year to visit this place is between the months of October to June. And while the clear skies and mild temperature will welcome you, during the month of January the temperature drops down. This time is also known for the famous annual Timket festival. During this time the locals wear their traditional dresses and priests carry sequined velvet umbrellas. This time of the year makes Ethiopia one of the best travel destinations in January.
